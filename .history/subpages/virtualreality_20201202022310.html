uTooth

Placing as a top five finalist for VR at MIT Reality Hack, uTooth is a framework for Bluetooth communication between Unity and external devices. This is a new tool that allows XR creators to implement peripherals and external devices fast and efficiently. See more of the project here on DevPost.
Within the project, my specific role was to work on scripting visual effects and controller mechanics, scene building, debugging, and VR configuration of our project for the Oculus Quest.
