ABCDerium

Click here to view the full spread. For this project, I developed a 56-page book consisting of 26 simple letter/ image relationships, arranged in alphabetical order, portraying 26 different typefaces. While working on this project, I focused on examining the visual relationships between type and images, and the degree to which one form can support and enhance the other, while considering the relationships between form, function, and meaning.
